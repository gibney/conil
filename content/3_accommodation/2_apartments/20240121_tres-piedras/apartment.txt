Description: Set in Conil de la Frontera, the property is about 1.4 km from Fuente del Gallo Beach, 19 km from Novo Sancti Petri Golf 
and 47 km from Genoves Park. The property is non-smoking and is situated 400 metres from Los Bateles Beach.
The spacious apartment comes with 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, bed linen, towels, TV with cable channels, a dining area, a fully 
equipped kitchen, and a balcony with sea views. Guests can take in the ambience of the surroundings from an outdoor dining area. 
Additional in-room amenities include wine or champagne.

Guests at the apartment will be able to enjoy activities in and around Conil de la Frontera, like fishing and hiking. The apartment 
complex has a picnic area where you can spend a day out in the open.

Club de Golf Campano is 12 km from Apartment Las Tres Piedras, while Benalup Golf & Country Club is 36 km away. The nearest 
airport is Jerez, 66 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.

Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 10 for a two-person trip.
