Intro:

Located in Roche area 5 Km from Conil. The campsite is surrounded by Pine 
forests and through them you reach the coast. There are multiple paths that cover 
this area and you can drive to Puntalejos beach at the Calas de Roche. 

This natural area is situated on the outskirts of Conil, only 7 minutes by car or bus from Conil.
